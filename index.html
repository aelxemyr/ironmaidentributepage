IRON MAIDEN

A tribute page by Alex Myers

ABOUT

Pioneers of the New Wave of British Heavy Metal (NWOBHM) sound, Iron Maiden combined the heavy-hitting sounds of hard rock and metal progenitors like Black Sabbath, Deep Purple, and Led Zeppelin with the complex musical sensibilities of progressive rock legends like Pink Floyd, Genesis, and Yes to create one of the most influential and recognizable metal legacies around. Founded in 1975 by bassist and songwriter Steve Harris, Iron Maiden have recorded 16 studio albums to date and are one of the most successful heavy metal bands of all time.

Their current line up consists of bassist and primary songwriter Steve Harris, vocalist Bruce Dickinson, guitarists Dave Murray, Adrian Smith, and Janick Gers, and drummer Nicko McBrain.
